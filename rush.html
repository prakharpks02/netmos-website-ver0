Booking a delivery with Rush is simple and quick. Once you download the app, just enter your pickup and drop-off location, choose from available transporters, and confirm your booking. You’ll receive a confirmation and be able to track your delivery in real-time.

Once your delivery is booked, the app provides a real-time tracking feature that allows you to monitor your shipment from pickup to delivery. You’ll receive live updates on its location and estimated delivery time, ensuring you’re always informed.

Rush operates across India, covering all major cities, towns, and rural areas. No matter where you need to send your shipment, Rush connects you with transporters to ensure it gets there safely and on time.

The transportation rates are calculated based on the distance between the pickup and delivery locations, the size/weight of your shipment, and the type of delivery service you choose. You can compare prices from different transporters before confirming your booking, ensuring transparent and competitive pricing.

Yes, with Rush, you can schedule deliveries in advance by selecting a preferred pickup date and time. This feature is ideal for businesses planning bulk shipments or individuals sending packages on specific dates.

Rush ensures your deliveries are safe and secure with multiple layers of protection, including OTP (One-Time Password) verification, image proof of delivery, and geotagging. You’ll also be able to track the shipment in real-time to monitor its progress.
