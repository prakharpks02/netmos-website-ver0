Pragati is a fleet and logistics management platform designed to streamline transportation operations. It helps businesses track vehicles in real time, optimize routes, monitor driver performance, and manage maintenance schedules. By using Pragati, you can reduce operational costs, improve efficiency, and enhance overall productivity.

Pragati uses GPS technology to provide real-time updates on your vehicles' locations. The platform allows you to monitor the progress of your fleet, view route history, and receive alerts for route deviations or delays, ensuring greater control and safety for your operations.

Yes, Pragati tracks driver performance through metrics like speed, idle time, and harsh braking. This data is compiled into detailed reports, helping you identify areas for improvement and reward top-performing drivers. Pragati’s maintenance tracker allows you to schedule vehicle servicing based on mileage, usage, or specific time intervals. Automated reminders are sent when maintenance is due, helping you avoid unexpected breakdowns and ensuring your fleet stays in optimal condition.
