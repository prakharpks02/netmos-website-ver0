Pragati Driver is a dedicated mobile app designed for drivers within the Pragati platform. It helps drivers manage routes, track deliveries, and communicate with transport managers seamlessly.

Pragati Driver simplifies the daily tasks of drivers by offering features like route navigation, real-time tracking, and automated updates about their trips and deliveries. It ensures that drivers have all the information they need at their fingertips.

Yes, the Pragati Driver app is free to download and use for drivers registered with companies that utilize the Pragati platform.

Drivers are assigned tasks directly through the app by their dispatchers or managers. They can view their upcoming tasks, including details of the delivery location and goods to be transported.

All data in the Pragati Driver app is secured with encryption, ensuring that sensitive information is protected from unauthorized access.
